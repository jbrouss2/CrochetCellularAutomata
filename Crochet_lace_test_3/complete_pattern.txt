Cellular Automata Crochet Pattern
Stitch definitions: dc = double crochet, ch = chain
Note: Please note that the ch 3 and turn counts as the first dc.
Foundation Row: ch 32.

Row 1: 30 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 2: 30 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 3: 6 dc, 4 ch, 4 dc, 2 ch, 4 dc, 4 ch, 6 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 4: (4 dc, 2 ch)*2, (2 dc, 2 ch)*2, 4 dc, 2 ch, 4 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 5: 4 dc, 2 ch, 6 dc, 6 ch, 6 dc, 2 ch, 4 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 6: 6 dc, 2 ch, (2 dc, 4 ch)*2, 2 dc, 2 ch, 6 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 7: 8 dc, 4 ch, 2 dc, 2 ch, 2 dc, 4 ch, 8 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 8: 4 dc, 6 ch, (2 dc, 6 ch)*2, 4 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 9: 8 dc, 4 ch, 2 dc, 2 ch, 2 dc, 4 ch, 8 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 10: 6 dc, 2 ch, (2 dc, 4 ch)*2, 2 dc, 2 ch, 6 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 11: 4 dc, 2 ch, 6 dc, 6 ch, 6 dc, 2 ch, 4 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 12: (4 dc, 2 ch)*2, (2 dc, 2 ch)*2, 4 dc, 2 ch, 4 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 13: 6 dc, 4 ch, 4 dc, 2 ch, 4 dc, 4 ch, 6 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 14: 30 dc, ch 3 and turn.

Row 15: 30 dc, ch 3 and turn.
